Are you ready
to take a leap of faith

and move to Fuerteventura?

If you want to move to Fuerteventura but don't know where or how to even begin, you're in the right place - Our mission is to document every step, every application proccess for residency and almost everything required to successfully relocate. Our goal is to serve as a guide to others who are planning to embark on this exciting journey.


Hi! I'm Grace, author of this blog and personally, my answer to the burning question above is
yes AND no!
Emotionally, yes! I'm super ready and super excited to move to Fuerteventura.
Mentally? No, not at all! Relocating to anywhere in the world is not easy, and I'm speaking from experience when I moved from the Phillipines to the UK. But back then, I was only 13 so a lot of the legalities and beurocracy were handled by my mum (thanks Ma!). This time, however, I have to fill out forms, compile legal documents and research the heck out of what needs to be done. It's so overwhelming, so much so that I feel like I need someone to hold my hand and guide me😩I'm sure that there lots of others out there who feel exactly like I do,
hence, the creation of fuerteventurafilipina.com

A little bit more about me...


Here's a question for you.... do you think the website looks a bit amateurish? That's because it is! I am not a web developer by trade but I am proud to say that I hand coded everything on this website. During Covid lockdown in 2020, while I was furloughed from my job at the time, I wanted to do something fun, like learn how to code. I watched Youtube tutorials and read hundreds of articles and that's how I learned the fundamentals of web development. You think this website is meh? You should see the first few websites that I created! (You can find them on my Instagram, if you fancy a little laugh!). Unfortunately, when the lockdown was over and it was time to face reality, ie. going back to work (booo!), I completely pushed my learning to the back of my priority list until fast forward to 2024 when I had a "Eureka" moment!
