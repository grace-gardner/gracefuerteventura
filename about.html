The How, What & Why of
fuerteventurafilipina.com


How did it begin?

First, let me ask you this - Do you think this website is
"amateur-ish"?
That's because it is! I am not a web developer by trade but I am proud to say that I hand coded everything on this website. During Covid lockdown in 2020, I was furloughed from my job at the time. I wanted to do something productive to pass the time. So, I started learning how to code. I watched Youtube tutorials, read articles online and learned the basics of web development.
If you think, this website is meh, you should see some of the "practice websites" that I created 😁 (You can find them on my Instagram page, if you fancy a bit of a laugh! 😂)
Anyway, when lockdown was over and it was time to face reality again, ie. going back to work (booo!), I completely pushed my learning to the bottom of my priority list until fast forward to 2024 when I had a "Eureka!" moment: I am going to create fuerteventurafilipina.com!

What is this website about?

Like I said, fast forward to 2024, my husband and I decided to move to Fuerteventura. Whilst doing our research on how to apply for residency, a lot of the information we found online were pretty generic to the whole of Spain. Sure, the process are the same but we wanted to know how to do it specifically in Fuerteventura, like, how and where on the island do we lodge the application? What and where can we get the documents and application forms from? We spent hours going through a lot of websites, forums, Facebook groups etc. It was frustrating 🥴
That's when I realised - if only there's a website where everything specific to residency in Fuerteventura can be found online.
fuerteventurafilipina.com is exactly that!
I got back into coding so I can create this amatuer (but unique) website which focuses specifically on relocating to Fuerteventura based on actual events that we would personally undertake and document.

Why create it?

As mentioned in our intro, if you are planning to relocate to Fuerteventura but don't where to start, we're here for you. With this website, we want to help you by way of providing a detailed "how-to-guide" to save you the hassle and hours of research so you can focus on what matters most - enoying your time on this paradise island!
